About

UI/UX Designer & Web Developer.

I always thought my dream was to be a Mechanical Engineer for a manufacturing company, preparing a design sheet and making the next generation machine. It seemed like an appropriate dream given my BE in Mechanical Engineering. I gravitated toward Mechanical Engineering positions. But I kind of just felt meh. I yearned to learn more, yearned to learn something different.

And that's when it happened. The spark I was missing ignited the instant I was recommended FSWD course and decided to pursue my career in IT. Since then, I can't get enough. Full-stack web development calls to all my passions; it incorporates creativity and problem solving. I love applying responsive design principles and watching my web pages shrink into mobile screens and still look amazing. It's oddly satisfying. I think in a way I'm kind of like those web pages; I'm moldable, but I still keep my creative flare intact.

My specialties include quickly learning new skills and programming languages, problem solving, domain driven design, responsive design principles. So far I have HTML, CSS , Javascript, React, Readux , Nodejs, express, MongoDb under my belt. I've started learning. I'm still enthusiastically grabbing onto any other programming languages, frameworks, or principles I can integrate into the coding web in my head.
